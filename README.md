# 🚉 Railway Footfall Prediction using Machine Learning

> **An AI-powered predictive analytics system that forecasts railway station passenger footfall to support smarter crowd management, operational planning, and resource optimization.**

---

## 📖 Overview

Railway stations experience fluctuating passenger volumes due to holidays, festivals, weekdays, weather conditions, and special events. Accurate prediction of passenger footfall enables railway authorities to allocate resources efficiently, improve passenger safety, and reduce congestion.

This project leverages **Machine Learning** techniques to analyze historical railway passenger data and predict future footfall trends. By identifying patterns in travel behavior, the system assists railway administrators in making informed operational decisions.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Managing passenger crowds at railway stations is challenging due to unpredictable variations in footfall. Inaccurate estimation may result in:

- Overcrowded platforms
- Long waiting times
- Insufficient staff allocation
- Safety risks during peak hours
- Inefficient train scheduling
- Poor passenger experience

Traditional planning methods rely on historical averages, which often fail to capture dynamic travel patterns.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

This project develops a **Machine Learning-based Railway Footfall Prediction System** that forecasts passenger volume using historical travel data and contextual features.

The model analyzes various influencing factors to provide accurate passenger count predictions, helping railway authorities improve operational efficiency and passenger safety.
